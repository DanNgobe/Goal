# Product Overview

A 5v5 tactical soccer game for Roblox where NPCs are the primary players and human players possess and control NPCs on their team.

## Core Mechanics

- **NPC-Centric Gameplay**: 10 NPCs (5 per team) play soccer autonomously
- **Player Possession**: Human players join teams and switch between NPCs to control them
- **Formation-Based AI**: NPCs follow tactical formations (Neutral, Attacking, Defensive) when not controlled
- **Team System**: Blue vs Red teams with auto-balancing
- **Dynamic Formations**: Teams shift formations based on ball possession

## Key Features

- Player switching (C key) to closest NPC on your team
- Ball possession and kick mechanics (ground/air kicks with charge system)
- AI behavior: passing, shooting, dribbling, and positional play
- Goal detection and scoring with intermission system
- Match timer with automatic round resets
- Kickoff system where defending team is frozen until ball is touched

## Player Experience

Players spectate initially, join a team (auto-balanced), then possess NPCs to play soccer. When not controlled, NPCs play tactically using AI. Players can switch between NPCs on their team to respond to game situations.
